About Me

Hi, my name is Kristjana and I’m an architect. I was born in Albania in 1994 but I've been living in Italy since 2003. I like to think that somehow or other my way had always been this but maybe initially it was not entirely clear. As a child I was fascinated by the monumental buildings, I was nut for the beautiful religious architecture, the timeless ones that make you feel tiny inside a space so suggestive, those that take your breath away leaving you with a single question: “How did they build something like that?”.

I often looked at houses and fantasized about how to build one. Then, these trivial observations began to evolve: at middle school, when I started the Technical Drawing lessons, I was really taken by working with set squares, sketch axonometries, drawing with precision; so, when I got to high school I chose to attend a Technical Institute for Surveyors where I deepened my knowledge in the building field and in which I get a diploma in surveying in 2013. But I could do more and still I was not satisfied with the aims I achieved so, the same year, I enrolled at the University of Florence to obtain a five-year degree in Architecture. Almost five years have passed since then and soon I will also have completed this educational path thanks of whom I’m better understanding witch results achieve in the future.

I am fascinated by all aspects of this subject, from urban planning to architectural design, especially the way of representing and communicating architecture. The latter is an aspect that I have approached only in the last two years but that I would like to study in the near future and to which I am starting to devote more attention with the intention of always improving myself.
